DirectChat User Guide
=====================

DirectChat allows you to open WhatsApp chats with any number without saving them.

Quick Start:
1. Select your country code.
2. Enter the phone number.
3. Write an optional pre-filled message.
4. Click "Send Message" to redirect to WhatsApp, or "Scan to Chat" to generate a QR Code.

Supported Platforms:
- Web browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Android (via APK/Capacitor native app)
- iOS (via IPA/Capacitor native app)

Features:
- Dark / Light theme.
- Clerk Auth Cloud Sync: syncs recent contact numbers across devices.
- Contacts Access: choose from device contacts.
- Recent Calls: load numbers from your phone's call history.
